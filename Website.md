# Driver Training Website

We need to build a modern mobile first website for our driver training business in the UK. We are 2 ex-paramedics who have trained and tested paramedics in order to obtain their C1 driver licence. We have an existing [site](https://www.c1drivertraining.com/) but it is focused on the C1 driver training side of our business and we've now purchased a new domain - drivertrainingnorthwest.co.uk - and we want to advertise that we train students to obtain either their C1 driving classification or to obtain their regular licence.

## Requirements

- To with [Decap](https://decapcms.org/docs/intro/) CMS
- CMS page templates to easily allow non-technical colleagues to update website content
- Mobile first design
- Modern looking wesbite
- SEO focused for:
  - both C1 and regular driver training
  - North West of England - Warrington / Manchester
- Performant
- FAQ section
  - Answer standard driver training questions and hit on SEO content keywords
- Search the web for example webistes and content
- Use https://ui.shadcn.com/ components
- USe all of NextJS functionality to optimise images were necessary
- Webiste will be deployed to Netlify so create a netlify.toml file
