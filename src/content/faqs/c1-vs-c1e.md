---
question: What's the difference between C1 and C1E?
category: C1 License
order: 4
---

C1 allows you to drive vehicles between 3.5-7.5 tonnes. C1E (C1 with trailer) allows you to drive C1 vehicles with a trailer over 750kg, where the combined weight doesn't exceed 12 tonnes. Most ambulance drivers only need the standard C1 license.
